Rule of Thirds

H&M

https://www2.hm.com/en_us/index.html

The rule of thirds in graphic design divides a canvas into three even rows and three even columns. The four central intersections where the lines meet are the key “hot spots” where you should aim to place your main subjects, as those are where people's attention immediately lands.
